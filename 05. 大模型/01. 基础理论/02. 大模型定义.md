**1.** **大模型的定义**  
  
**大模型是指具有大规模参数和复杂计算结构的机器学习模型。**这些模型通常由[深度神经网络](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E6%2525B7%2525B1%2525E5%2525BA%2525A6%2525E7%2525A5%25259E%2525E7%2525BB%25258F%2525E7%2525BD%252591%2525E7%2525BB%25259C%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)构建而成，拥有数十亿甚至数千亿个参数。大模型的设计目的是为了提高模型的表达能力和预测性能，能够处理更加复杂的任务和数据。大模型在各种领域都有广泛的应用，包括[自然语言处理](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E8%252587%2525AA%2525E7%252584%2525B6%2525E8%2525AF%2525AD%2525E8%2525A8%252580%2525E5%2525A4%252584%2525E7%252590%252586%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)、计算机视觉、语音识别和[推荐系统](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E6%25258E%2525A8%2525E8%25258D%252590%2525E7%2525B3%2525BB%2525E7%2525BB%25259F%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)等。大模型通过训练海量数据来学习复杂的模式和特征，具有更强大的[泛化能力](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E6%2525B3%25259B%2525E5%25258C%252596%2525E8%252583%2525BD%2525E5%25258A%25259B%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)，可以对未见过的数据做出准确的预测。  
  
[ChatGPT](https://zhida.zhihu.com/search?content_id=238331534&content_type=Article&match_order=1&q=ChatGPT&zhida_source=entity) 对大模型的解释更为通俗易懂，也更体现出类似人类的归纳和思考能力：大模型本质上是一个使用海量数据训练而成的深度[神经网络模型](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E7%2525A5%25259E%2525E7%2525BB%25258F%2525E7%2525BD%252591%2525E7%2525BB%25259C%2525E6%2525A8%2525A1%2525E5%25259E%25258B%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)，其巨大的数据和参数规模，实现了智能的涌现，展现出类似人类的智能。



**5.** **大模型的分类**  
  
按照输入数据类型的不同，大模型主要可以分为以下三大类：  

![](https://pica.zhimg.com/v2-442579f2183a20c335729d2e0276a93c_1440w.jpg)

  
**· [语言大模型](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E8%2525AF%2525AD%2525E8%2525A8%252580%2525E5%2525A4%2525A7%2525E6%2525A8%2525A1%2525E5%25259E%25258B%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)（NLP）**：是指在自然语言处理（Natural Language Processing，NLP）领域中的一类大模型，通常用于处理文本数据和理解自然语言。这类大模型的主要特点是它们在大规模语料库上进行了训练，以学习自然语言的各种语法、语义和语境规则。例如：GPT 系列（OpenAI）、Bard（Google）、[文心一言](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E6%252596%252587%2525E5%2525BF%252583%2525E4%2525B8%252580%2525E8%2525A8%252580%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)（百度）。  
  
**· [视觉大模型](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E8%2525A7%252586%2525E8%2525A7%252589%2525E5%2525A4%2525A7%2525E6%2525A8%2525A1%2525E5%25259E%25258B%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)（CV）**：是指在计算机视觉（Computer Vision，CV）领域中使用的大模型，通常用于图像处理和分析。这类模型通过在大规模图像数据上进行训练，可以实现各种视觉任务，如[图像分类](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E5%25259B%2525BE%2525E5%252583%25258F%2525E5%252588%252586%2525E7%2525B1%2525BB%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)、目标检测、[图像分割](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E5%25259B%2525BE%2525E5%252583%25258F%2525E5%252588%252586%2525E5%252589%2525B2%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)、[姿态估计](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E5%2525A7%2525BF%2525E6%252580%252581%2525E4%2525BC%2525B0%2525E8%2525AE%2525A1%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)、人脸识别等。例如：VIT 系列（Google）、[文心UFO](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xie.infoq.cn/link%3Ftarget%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fwww.zhihu.com%252Fsearch%253Fq%253D%2525E6%252596%252587%2525E5%2525BF%252583UFO%2526search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_source%253DEntity%2526hybrid_search_extra%253D%25257B%252522sourceType%252522%25253A%252522answer%252522%25252C%252522sourceId%252522%25253A3271533824%25257D)、华为盘古 CV、INTERN（商汤）。  
  
**· [多模态大模型](https://zhida.zhihu.com/search?content_id=238331534&content_type=Article&match_order=1&q=%E5%A4%9A%E6%A8%A1%E6%80%81%E5%A4%A7%E6%A8%A1%E5%9E%8B&zhida_source=entity)：**是指能够处理多种不同类型数据的大模型，例如文本、图像、音频等多模态数据。这类模型结合了 NLP 和 CV 的能力，以实现对多模态信息的综合理解和分析，从而能够更全面地理解和处理复杂的数据。例如：DingoDB 多模向量数据库（九章云极 DataCanvas）、DALL-E(OpenAI)、悟空画画（华为）、midjourney。
